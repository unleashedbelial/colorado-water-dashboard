📋 About This Data

Sources & methodology

Reservoir levels (Lake Powell & Mead) from USBR Hydrologic Database (HDB) — daily observations updated each morning.

Streamflow is real-time from USGS NWIS, updated every 15–60 min. Values in cubic feet per second (cfs).

Snowpack from NRCS SNOTEL at Grand Mesa, CO (10,600 ft), updated daily. SWE = snow water equivalent in inches.

Lees Ferry, AZ is the legal accounting point dividing the Upper and Lower Colorado Basin under the 1922 Colorado River Compact.
