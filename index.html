📋 About This Data

Sources & methodology

Streamflow data is real-time from USGS National Water Information System (NWIS), updated every 15–60 minutes. Values in cubic feet per second (cfs).

Snowpack is from NRCS SNOTEL network at Grand Mesa, CO (elev. 10,600 ft), updated daily. SWE = snow water equivalent in inches.

Lees Ferry, AZ is the legal accounting point separating the Upper and Lower Colorado River Basin under the 1922 Colorado River Compact.
